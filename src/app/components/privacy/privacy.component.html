Privacy Policy & Terms of Service

Borg El Arab Technological University - Graduation Project Platform

Last Updated: October 26, 2024

1. Commitment to Privacy

Borg El Arab Technological University ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting the privacy and security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your data when you use our Graduation Project Management Platform (the "Platform"). This document also serves as our Terms of Service.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information necessary for the academic and administrative functions of the Platform:

  • Identity & Academic Data: Full name, student/faculty ID, university email, department, and faculty affiliation.
  • Project Data: Project titles, abstracts, proposals, source code, datasets, reports, presentations, and any other submitted files. This includes metadata such as submission timestamps and version history.
  • Collaboration Data: Team member assignments, supervisor-student communications, comments, and progress logs.
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, operating system, and session logs for security and performance monitoring. We do not use tracking cookies for advertising purposes.

3. Purpose of Data Use (Terms of Service)

By using the Platform, you agree that your data will be used for the following academic purposes:

  • Project Management: To facilitate the submission, review, evaluation, and grading of graduation projects.
  • Academic Integrity: To use automated tools for plagiarism detection and ensure the originality of submitted work.
  • Internal Archiving: To maintain a secure, internal digital archive of university projects for historical and reference purposes.
  • Communication: To send official notifications regarding deadlines, feedback, and other platform-related updates.
  • Platform Improvement: To analyze anonymized usage patterns to enhance platform functionality and user experience.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

Your information is treated with strict confidentiality and is shared only under the following circumstances:

  • Within the University: Data is accessible to authorized personnel, including your project supervisors, department heads, and academic committee members, for evaluation and administrative purposes only.
  • Plagiarism Detection Services: Submitted documents may be securely transmitted to third-party plagiarism-checking services under a strict data processing agreement.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to a valid legal request from a public authority.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information with any third parties for commercial purposes.

5. Data Security Measures

We implement robust technical and organizational measures to protect your data, including end-to-end encryption for data in transit, access control protocols, regular security audits, and data backup and recovery plans.

6. User Rights and Responsibilities

  • Access and Correction: You have the right to access and update your personal profile information through the Platform's settings.
  • Intellectual Property: You and your team members retain the intellectual property rights to your original work. However, by submitting your project, you grant the university a non-exclusive, perpetual, royalty-free license to use, reproduce, and archive the project for non-commercial, educational purposes.
  • Acceptable Use: You agree not to use the Platform for any unlawful purpose, to upload malicious software, or to infringe upon the rights of others.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. Your continued use of the Platform after such changes constitutes your acceptance of the new terms.
